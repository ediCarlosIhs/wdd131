About

Thank you for visiting my webpage. The messages are generate in a aleatory way, but I do a relative complex calculation to show you a message acordily the time you enter the website or click the next button.

I use Unix Timestamp (Epoch Time). Unix Timestamp is the miliseconds count from the midnight January 1, 1970.

To make it a little more complex, I get the seconds, multiply it by 7 (a special meaning number in the Bible), and add the timestamp at the moment you visit the page.
